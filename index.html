Percy Jackson and the lightning thief (2010)

Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief is a action fantasy film directed by Chris Columbus from a screenplay by Craig Titley, based on the 2005 novel The Lightning Thief by Rick Riordan.
A teenager discovers he's the descendant of a Greek god and sets out on an adventure to settle an on-going battle between the gods.
